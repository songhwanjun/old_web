Biography

Currently, I am a Research Scientist at NAVER AI Lab. I graduated with my PhD in Feburary 2021 from the Graduate School of Knowledge Service Engineering at KAIST under the supervision of Prof. Jae-Gil Lee, who is leading Data Mining Lab.

Last year, I worked as a research intern (Google Research, 2020 June – December) under the supervision of two hosts, Eunyoung Kim and Ming-Hsuan Yang, and two mentors, Dr. Deqing Sun and Dr. Varun Jampani.

My general research interests lie in improving the performance of machine learning (ML) techniques under real-world scenarios. I am particularly interested in designing more advanced approaches to handle large-scale and noisy data, which are two main real-world challenges to hinder the practical use of ML approaches. So far, I published 4 KDD (including 1 tutorial proposal), 2 ICML (including 1 workshop paper), 1 SIGMOD, 1 AAAI, 1 WWW, 1 CIKM, 1 PAKDD, 1 ML Journal.

In NAVER AI Lab, there are many open positions for research internship. If you are interested in our group, please see my previous papaers and send an email to me with your CV and research interest (contact: hwanjun.song@navercorp.com).
